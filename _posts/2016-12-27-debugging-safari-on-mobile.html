How to setting

  1. Go to 'General' --> 'Safari' --> 'Advanced' --> turn on 'Web inspector'
  2. Open Safari Browser on your device
  3. Connect your device to Mac with USB.
  4. Open Safari Browser on Mac and select 'Develop' on menu bar --> select your device
  5. Select website that you would like to inspect.
